E-Sports (hereinafter referred to as ES) is an application to play e-sports games. Users can only mint NFT by consuming $ES. Players can get rewards by playing different games via mobile phones or PCs, the more time you played the more reward you earned. Players will get extra rewards by getting MVP or SVP in each game.

Wide Range
For millions of players who loved e-sports, there is no limit on age, on gender, you can participate as long as you love e-sports.
Easy to Start
The players only need to download the ES app. By doing this, you can get both entertainment and money.
High Security
Es are committed to connect the mature esports games on the market in stead of developing our own games, which will make the project more fun and safety.
Powerful Marketing
The team focuses on the marketing of the highly developed e-sports in the Asia-Pacific region. They have begun the negotiation about the promotion of large-scale internet cafes and the cooperation between major e-sports clubs in China and South Korea.
Play to earn
Different from traditional play to earn projects, ES only need players to connect the 3A games or famous games like League of Legend with ES app, players can get rewards after the game story uploaded. Say goodbye to the fake games, welcome back to the real game world.
Staking
Token holders can stake their $ES and receive compound rewards via the Play-To-Earn agreement, which combined with $BUSD and $ES. There is a flexible time lock-in in our contract, which means that the longer you stake, the more you earn.
NFT
Different with traditional NFTs, there are five different levels in ES NFT. This corresponds to the difference in Luck point and Endurance point. The chance that players can get extra rewards will be higher if the luck point of the NFTs they owned are higher. The Endurance point will be reduced when players are getting rewards by play to earn. The NFT will be destroyed after the Endurance point reduced to zero. The value of the NFT will keep increase since there is a limit in mint NFTs.
